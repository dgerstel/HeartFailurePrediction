Heart failure prognostics

Welcome to the web app that predicts survival within 100 days after a heart failure episode.
  • On the left you can see distributions of characteristics of the patients that survived (blue) and passed away (red) within a follow-up period. On the bottom left you can also see charts of binary characteristics (e.g. whether they had anaemia, 1=yes, 0=no) combined with whether they survived ("0") or not ("1").
  • On the right you can fill out a form with your patient characteristics and the Machine Learning algorithm (Random Forests) will predict survival within the next 100 days. The algorithm has roughly 70% accuracy and is meant as a demonstration only and should not be used for real-life cases (which would require further checks from the data science and medical perspectives).
